FAQ

What can be personalized?

Names, date, and a short phrase. You’ll choose from a few curated font/style options to keep everything consistent and high-quality.

Do you offer rush orders?

Yes when possible. Rush fees may apply depending on timeline and current queue.

Can I match my wedding colors?

Usually yes for the ring box (PLA color options). For plaques, wood tone and acrylic style options vary.

What’s your return/refund policy?

Because items are personalized, returns aren’t typically accepted. If something arrives damaged or incorrect, I’ll make it right.
